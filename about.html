Welcome to the IronZoo Production's Website

Who are we? Well IronZoo is a group of Florida State students, who have united for the common goal of producing, marketing, and distributing high-quality media. Our founding members met through the FSU media production program and were admirers of each other’s works. We banded together to focus on producing enticing visuals and thoughtful storytelling. After founding the initial group we branched out and acquired members from various fields, such as marketing, business, web design, literature, and studio art. Our goal is to produce creative films and shows for the Internet age.

We have created a variety of different projects in the year we’ve been around. These include: web shows, documentaries, PSAs, and short films. You can see them for yourself here. After completing our narrative coursework, we took the initiative and created a Directed Independent Study course in association with the FSU College of Communication & Information. Our goal is to turn IronZoo Productions into a real production company. These past 3 months the team has been hard at work, creating our biggest project yet, Chronos.
